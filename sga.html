S Graduate Attributes

My Learning Skills

These helped me acquire the ability to learn and effectively apply knowledge which is essential for the understanding a subject and fulfilling academic potential , these Skills might be also needed and can be applied in other areas of work place

Examples: Academic Skills, Applying knowledge, Research and critical thinking, Digital capability.

My impact

Skills such as communication and collaboration while being considerate and including diversity , and being able to positively contribute and affect communities , these skills also regard the impact of ethical , societal and environmental decisions and are highly useful in my opinion when working with a team of people with diverse backgrounds

Examples: Interpersonal skills, Working with others, Equality and inclusion, Ethics and Sustainability.

Myself

The ability to build and maintain positive self care and to understand one's purpose supports the development of a lot of other skills and attributes and can help support lifelong and professional wellbeing these skills deepened my understanding to personal development and different aspects of it

Examples: Positive Wellbeing , Purpose, Personal development, Enterprising.

My Skills

Myskills portfolio is an innovative way to test , note , develop and review your skills on your own profile, Everything you record on your profile will be unique so it can be conveniently used on your CV to improve your job applications and performance in interviews.

Myskills also provides links to learning resources that support development and suggests actions that can help you develop your skills to achieve your goals.

My experience with Myskills is that it's a great way to keep track of areas you have improved in As well as it’s a good tool to keep track of what you’ve actually learned throughout the year / our degree , which saves you so much time when writing your CV.

More detailed information about MySkills : https://www.(aaaa).ac.uk/skills/myskills)
